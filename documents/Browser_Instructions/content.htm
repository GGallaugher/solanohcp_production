The following instructions allow you to adjust the size of the browser for optimal viewing:



Chrome

Click the icon in the top right corner of your browser, then set the zoom to 100%.




Internet Explorer

Click the gear icon in the top right corner of your browser, then select zoom, then select 100%.



Firefox

In the menu bar, click the View tab, then go to Zoom, then select Reset.




Safari

Click the menu icon in the top right corner of your browser, then go to zoom, and select Actual Size.
